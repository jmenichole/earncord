FAQ

Common questions

Straight answers. No earnings guarantees.

How do payouts work?

Verified survey completes credit your EarnCord balance. When you meet the minimum withdrawal and stay under daily caps, you can withdraw in USDT to a wallet you control. Processing depends on the payment provider and network — not on Discord.

Why didn’t I get paid for a survey?

Common reasons: you were screened out, the session didn’t complete on the partner side, or the postback hasn’t arrived yet. EarnCord credits from partner callbacks, not from closing the browser tab. Screen-outs never pay.

What is a screen-out?

The survey partner decided you weren’t a fit (demographics, quotas, or quality checks). No reward. Repeated screen-outs can lower your SurveyScore.

Is EarnCord part of Discord?

No. EarnCord is an independent product that uses Discord’s APIs and OAuth. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Discord Inc.

Do I have to use the website and the bot?

Yes for a full account: the website captures Privacy / Terms / 18+ consent and Discord login; the bot handles /start, matching, and day-to-day commands. See How it works.

How do I raise my score?

Complete your profile, finish surveys you qualify for, keep screen-outs down, and stay active over time. Details and tier cutoffs are on the SurveyScore page.
