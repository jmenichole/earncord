User journey

How EarnCord works

Six steps from first click to wallet. Surveys are not guaranteed — inventory, eligibility, and partner decisions vary.

  1. Web consent On the EarnCord site, accept Privacy and Terms, confirm you are 18+, then continue with Discord OAuth. That creates an active consent trail before any survey activity.
  2. Account link After login you land on Account. The site registers your Discord identity with the EarnCord API so the bot and web session share one user record.
  3. Discord /start Join a server with the EarnCord bot and run /start. Finish profile fields (age range, gender, country, interests). Profile completeness feeds matching and SurveyScore.
  4. Match When inventory fits your profile and score, the bot surfaces surveys in Discord (list commands and/or DMs). You open a partner survey link — not a random offerwall grind.
  5. Complete → credit Finish the survey with the partner. EarnCord credits your balance only after a verified server-to-server postback. Screen-outs and incomplete attempts do not pay; they can still affect your score.
  6. USDT withdraw When your balance clears the minimum and daily caps, withdraw USDT to a wallet you control via the payment provider. Timing depends on network and provider processing — not instant magic.

Why web first?

Discord alone is a weak place to capture Privacy / Terms / age consent. The web login gate records that acceptance, then OAuth ties the Discord account. The bot handles the day-to-day reward loop where members already hang out.

What you should expect

  • Not every survey will qualify you — partners screen for demographics and quality.
  • Credits wait on partner callbacks, not on clicking “done.”
  • Higher SurveyScore tiers can improve match priority and payout share rules — they are not a guarantee of more surveys.
