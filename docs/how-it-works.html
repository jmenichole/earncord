How it works

Six beats. No fluff.

  1. Consent on the web Accept Privacy, Terms, and 18+. Log in with Discord.
  2. Account links Your Discord ID is registered so the site and bot share one account.
  3. Discord /start Finish your profile in a server with the EarnCord bot.
  4. Match When a survey fits, the bot shows it — you open the partner link.
  5. Credit Balance updates only after the partner’s server confirms a complete.
  6. Withdraw USDT Cash out when you hit the minimum. Network timing varies.
