Partner-facing

For publishers & reviewers

Short overview for TheoremReach, CPX, Pollfish, and similar review teams evaluating EarnCord as a Discord-native survey panel.

Product summary

EarnCord matches Discord community members to paid survey inventory. Members consent on the public website, complete profile onboarding in Discord, and receive survey opportunities through the bot. Rewards credit only after server-to-server postbacks from the survey partner. Payouts to members are in USDT via a crypto payment provider.

Age gate & consent

  • 18+ — required checkbox before Discord OAuth; Terms and Privacy also require active acceptance.
  • Web registration ties Discord identity to that consent before survey matching begins.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 (see Privacy Policy).

Discord-native panel

  • Members live in Discord servers where the EarnCord bot is installed.
  • Onboarding via /start collects age range, gender, country, and interests for matching.
  • Survey offers surface in Discord; completion happens on the partner’s survey experience.
  • EarnCord is independent software using Discord’s platform APIs — not a Discord Inc. product.

Server-to-server postbacks

Completes and screen-outs are reconciled through partner callbacks (hash/HMAC verification where the partner supports it). Member balances update from verified events, not from client-side “I finished” claims. Exact callback paths and secrets are configured in the SurveyScore backend at deploy time — not published on this site.

Quality scoring

EarnCord runs an internal SurveyScore (0–1000) from completion rate, screen-out rate, profile completeness, tenure, and recent consistency. Higher tiers can influence matching priority and payout share rules. This is a publisher-side quality signal, not a guarantee of respondent volume or CPI.

Technical stack (high level)

  • Static site (GitHub Pages) + Cloudflare Worker for Discord OAuth
  • SurveyScore API + Discord bot (Node) for ledger, matching, and postbacks
  • PostgreSQL for accounts, survey history, and withdrawals

Operator deploy details: github.com/jmenichole/surveyscore and github.com/jmenichole/earncord.
