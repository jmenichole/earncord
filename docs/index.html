Surveys that come to you.

EarnCord is a Discord-native rewards product: web consent, bot matching, partner-verified completes, and USDT withdraws. No endless offerwalls. No fake earnings promises.

How it works Web consent → Discord /start → match → complete → credit → withdraw SurveyScore 0–1000 quality score, factor weights, bronze / silver / gold tiers For publishers Panel overview for TheoremReach and partner reviewers FAQ Payouts, screen-outs, Discord affiliation, contact

What EarnCord is

Members sign in on the web, accept Privacy and Terms, and confirm they are 18+. The Discord bot finishes profile onboarding, matches inventory to that profile and score, and credits balances only after survey partners post back a verified complete.

EarnCord is an independent product. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Discord Inc.
